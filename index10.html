History

Fishing With Jorgos is a Finnish fishing team founded in 2018. Our team has 3 members, we all share the healthy obsession to hiking and fishing. We Fish mostly pike, perch and zander. Once a year we take a trip to Finnish lapland, where we fish trout and salmon. Twice a year, we take part on the biggest pike fishing contest in Finland called HK-OPEN

Our visio

We have a vision to become one of the biggest influencers on the fishing market. We believe in the use of renewable resources in the manufacture of fishing and camping equipment. We want to organize free fishing trips for today's young people, and thereby teach responsibility, both in fishing and in everyday life. In the near future we will start manufactoring handmade lures and flies. We also have a dream of hosting our own fishing competition some day.
